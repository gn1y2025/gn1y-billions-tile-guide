## Important

Do not share:

- seed phrase;
- private key;
- wallet files;
- `kms.json`;
- identity backup files;
- browser profile data;
- full screenshots with sensitive wallet/account data.

When asking for help, use:

- [Support question template](../templates/support-question-template.md)
