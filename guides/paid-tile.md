# Paid Tile Guide

Safety-first guide for users who intentionally want to claim a paid Billions AI Agentic Movie Tile.

This guide is unofficial. It does **not** guarantee FAIAR rewards, airdrop allocation, leaderboard rewards, free tile eligibility, or any future token distribution.

Default guide mode is still:

**FREE CLAIM ONLY**

Paid tile claim is optional and spends real funds.

---

## What paid tile means

Paid tile claim means the user intentionally wants to spend real funds, usually shown as:

```text
10 USDC
```

or possibly:

```text
amount=10000000
```

`amount=10000000` may represent 10 USDC using 6 decimals.

This must be treated as a paid action.

---

## Paid claim is not required

Paid claim is not required for using this guide.

Paid claim does not guarantee:

- FAIAR rewards;
- airdrop allocation;
- leaderboard rewards;
- special eligibility;
- profit;
- refund.

Only continue if the human clearly understands the risk.

---

## Required explicit confirmation

An AI agent must not continue with paid claim unless the human writes exactly:

```text
I understand this is a paid claim and I want to spend 10 USDC.
```

Without this exact confirmation:

```text
STOP.
```

Before paid claim, confirm:

```text

Token: USDC
Network: shown by official flow
Official claim page: confirmed
Agent address: correct
DID: correct
Human link: verified
Payment endpoint / recipient: official
Wallet: separate wallet, not main wallet
```

The human must review this summary before continuing.

---

## Stop conditions

Stop immediately if:

- the human wanted free claim;
- the amount is not expected;
- the token is not USDC;
- the network looks wrong;
- the website looks unofficial;
- the wallet asks for seed phrase;
- the wallet asks for private key;
- the wallet asks for unlimited approval;
- DID is wrong;
- agent address is wrong;
- human link is not verified;
- the user does not understand that this spends real funds;
- the user did not explicitly confirm paid claim.

---

## Wallet safety

Use a separate wallet for paid tile activity.

Do not use a wallet with valuable assets.

Do not paste seed phrase or private key into:

- terminal;
- website;
- Discord;
- Telegram;
- AI agent chat;
- GitHub issue;
- Google form;
- any unknown script.
