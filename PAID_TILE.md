# Paid Tile Advanced Section

Paid tile claim is optional and spends real funds.

Default guide mode is **FREE CLAIM ONLY**.

Paid claim is allowed only if the human clearly confirms that they understand this is a paid action and want to spend 10 USDC.

Paid claims do not guarantee FAIAR rewards.

## Stop conditions

Stop immediately if:

- amount is not expected;
- token is not USDC;
- network looks wrong;
- website looks unofficial;
- wallet asks for seed phrase;
- wallet asks for private key;
- wallet asks for unlimited approval;
- DID or agent address is wrong;
- human did not explicitly confirm paid claim.
